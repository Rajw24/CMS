Hello this is Course Management System

Vision

A course management system (CMS), also known as a learning management system (LMS), is a software platform used by educational institutions to manage and deliver educational courses online. CMSs typically provide a range of features and tools to support the creation, delivery, and assessment of online courses, as well as to facilitate communication and collaboration among teachers and students.

Mission

One of the primary functions of a CMS is to deliver course content and materials to students online. This can include multimedia content such as videos and interactive simulations, as well as written materials such as lectures, handouts, and assignments. CMSs may also offer tools for online assessment, such as quizzes and exams, and may provide automated grading and feedback.
